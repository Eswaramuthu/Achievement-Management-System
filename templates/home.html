Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Achievement Management System?

It is a centralized platform designed to help students and teachers track, verify, and display academic achievements efficiently.

Who can upload and verify achievements?

Students can view their profile and achievements, while authorized teachers record and verify achievements in seconds.

How does duplicate certificate detection work?

When a certificate is uploaded, the system computes its SHA-256 hash. If another entry matches this hash, the duplicate is detected and blocked.

Can I use this for placement interviews?

Absolutely. The system generates structured dashboards and downloadable formats to easily showcase your achievements to recruiters.
