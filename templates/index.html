Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What is the Achievement Management System?

It is a centralized platform that stores and organizes all student achievements in one place, making tracking and showcasing accomplishments simple and efficient.

Q2. How does this system help students?

Students get a single dashboard to view certificates, positions, and participation details, helping them quickly present their profiles during placements or applications.

Q3. How can teachers add achievements?

Teachers use easy forms with auto-complete to enter event information, upload certificates, and maintain accurate records in minutes.

Q4. What types of achievements are supported?

The system records hackathons, coding contests, conferences, paper presentations, symposiums, workshops, and other academic activities.

Q5. Can students submit achievements on their own?

Yes, students can submit entries if allowed, which can then be reviewed and approved by faculty for authenticity.

Q6. How is the information accessed?

All data is available through a clean, searchable dashboard where users can filter by category, date, or event type.
