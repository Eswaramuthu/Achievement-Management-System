Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 12 February 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how the Achievement Management System uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to improve user experience and ensure secure operation of the platform.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and enhance functionality.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login, authentication, and security
  • Preference Cookies: Store theme and display settings
  • Session Cookies: Maintain active user sessions
  • Analytics Cookies (if enabled): Help analyze platform usage

3. Use of Local Storage

We may use browser localStorage to store non-sensitive preferences such as dark/light mode settings and interface customization.

4. How Long Cookies Are Stored

Some cookies are deleted automatically when you close your browser, while others may remain until manually removed or expired.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

6. Third-Party Cookies

The platform does not intentionally use third-party cookies for advertising. Any third-party services used will comply with privacy regulations.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically. All changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions regarding this Cookie Policy, please contact the system administrator through official communication channels.
